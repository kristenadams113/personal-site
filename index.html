A Little About Me:

Hello everyone! My name is Kristen, and I am a rising senior at a STEM high school in New Jersey. When I'm not learning how to code, I am either listening to a new audiobook on my back porch or watching Netflix movies. I am obsessed with making music playlists on Spotify, and I have been playing piano for almost 13 years! My favorite STEM subject is biology because the concept of life and death fascinates me more than anything in the world, but I am also extremely fascinated by computers and how they are able to create projects from code!
