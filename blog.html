My Piano Journey

August 12, 2020

Thirteen years ago, I learned how to play the piano for the first time. I had my ups, and I had my downs. I threatened to quit multiple times throughout the years. But in the end, I grew to love the instrument and the different genres of music. Two years ago, I started a local piano lessons business, offering affordable lessons to children in my neighborhood. Since then, the business has grown to cover three towns, fifteen students, and ages ranging from 5 to 15! I am so happy to have the opportunity to share the gift of music with others. I also get to learn more about piano and myself through teaching!
